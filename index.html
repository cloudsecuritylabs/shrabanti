Continents, One Voice

Rooted in the living tradition of Rabindra Sangeet, Ankan brings the poetry and music of Nobel laureate Rabindranath Tagore to listeners across North America and beyond.

Trained in the classical Visva-Bharati style, Ankan blends scholarly depth with a performer's natural warmth — making these century-old songs feel urgently present. Based in Cumming, Georgia, he teaches students of all ages both in-person and online, building a new generation of Rabindrasangeet practitioners far from Tagore's own shores.

His recordings capture the meditative stillness, the monsoon longing, and the spiritual expansiveness that make Tagore's compositions utterly singular in the world's musical heritage.
