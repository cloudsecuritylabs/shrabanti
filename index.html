About Shrabanti

Shrabanti Basu is an experienced Rabindrasangeet artist, trained under Swapna Ghosal, and has been teaching and performing for many years. She performs across North America at cultural festivals, baithaks, and community events.

She has released three Rabindrasangeet albums — এ পরবাসে (2023), নিদ্রাবিহীন (2024), and গানের পরে গান (2025) — each reflecting her commitment to purity of sur, clarity of pronunciation, and emotional depth.

Shrabanti teaches Rabindrasangeet locally in Cumming, Georgia, and offers online lessons across North America.
