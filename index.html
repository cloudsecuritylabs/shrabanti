Songs to Sing

Shrabanti Basu is a devoted Rabindrasangeet artist and founder of সুরসন্ধান — a music school and practice dedicated to keeping Tagore's living tradition flourishing far from Bengal's shores.

Based in Cumming, Georgia, Shrabanti teaches students of all ages across India, North America, and beyond — both online and in person. Her teaching carries scholarly depth and the emotional warmth that Rabindrasangeet demands, welcoming beginners and advanced students alike into the world of Tagore's poetry and melody.

Her three albums — এ পরবাসে (2023), নিদ্রাবিহীন (2024), and গানের পরে গান (2025) — explore the longing of diaspora, the stillness of sleepless nights, and the joy of song following song.
